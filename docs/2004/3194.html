Thanks to everyone who replied - problem solved.

I changed

    (eval-when (:compile-toplevel :load-toplevel :execute)
      (require "comm"))

to

    (eval-when (:compile-toplevel)
      (require "com"))

in SLIME's swank-lispworks.lisp, moved (require "comm") to my deliver.lisp,
recompiled SLIME, delivered, and it worked!

Thanks again - Lisp is way cool.
