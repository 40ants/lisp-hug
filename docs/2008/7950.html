Hi

I am new to Lisp and LispWorks, but I am 
enjoying it tremendously... ;-)

I am working through the CAPI User Guide 
(of LispWorks 5.1 for Mac OS X) and in section 6.4.3.2
there seems to be an error.

The documentation shows this code:

(apply-in-pane-process 
 'set-hint-table editor2 '(:visible-min-width (:character 100))) 

but it pops up the debugger

I changed it to this:

(apply-in-pane-process
 editor2
 'set-hint-table editor2 '(:visible-min-width (:character 100)))

and it now works.

Hopefully someone from LispWorks will read this 
post and adjust the documentation.
