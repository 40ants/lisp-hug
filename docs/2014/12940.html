I have a p4 binding, here: <http://www.nicklevine.org/lw-p4/>

This sets editor keybindings as for a subset of p4.el:

     control-x p e   "P4 Edit"
     control-x p S   "P4 Submit"
     control-x p o   "P4 Opened"
     control-x p r   "P4 Revert"
