Cross-Platform Delivery

A paid-for LispWorks license allows for free of charge delivery of 
runtime applications.

LispWorks makes it possible to build an application that runs on 
Windows, Macintosh and Unix.

The question now is: if I buy a license for LispWorks on Mac OS X and I 
want to deploy on any of the other two platforms - Windows or Unix, can 
I do that somehow, or do I have to buy extra licenses just to deploy ? 
Or is there a special solution for this situation ?

I'm not Xanalys but I've asked the same question some time ago and the
answer was: You have to buy a license for each platform you want to
deploy on.

I think this is pretty reasonable given LW's fair price and the fact
that all three licenses come with unlimited distribution rights for
your applications.
