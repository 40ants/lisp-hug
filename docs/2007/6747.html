If your requirements are just to accomplish what you described above,  
you can use straight Lisp.  It doesn't get much simpler:

(defun rfs (string)
   (read-from-string (substitute #\. #\, string)))


> CL-USER> (read-from-string "1,2")
> 1.2
> 3

Cl-USER 291 > (rfs "1,2")
1.2
3
