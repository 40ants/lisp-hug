Re: Getting error trying to open file on new install

Hi Michal and others,

   I had this same problem after updating to MacOSX Sierra - very frustrating.

Turns out the CURRENT download of Personal Edition 6.1.1 is DIFFERENT than it was
when I downloaded over a year ago!   Deleting old LWPE 6.1.1 and re-downloading
and re-installing fixed the crash problems.

I'm certainly glad that LW fixed the bugs / incompatibilities, but I think it's extremely
disappointing that they didn't change the minor update number to at least 6.1.2.

I had contacted LW tech support and this is what Martin Simmons had to say:

"We have updated the Personal Edition 6.1.1. to fix this, so please try
redownloading and installing.  It isn't a new release, so it is still labeled
as 6.1.1"

The least they could do is put this note on the web site, but I think it's quite
poor form to change a product (even a free one) without changing the "numbers"
or indicating that there had been a change -- what about 6.1.1a , at the least?

Hope this helps some of you that are running into this problem on MacOS.

Best,
--Glenn
