MCLIDE 1.0b2 for LispWorks

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

MCLIDE is a free open source Macintosh IDE for LispWorks and other  
lisp implementations on any platform. It's a double-clickable, stand- 
alone development environment that connects to lisp systems either  
locally or through the network. The second public beta of MCLIDE 1.0  
is available from:

      http://mclide.in-progress.com

The new version of MCLIDE now supports LispWorks as target lisp,  
including LispWorks 6.0. MCLIDE also works with Clozure CL, Steel  
Banks Common Lisp (SBCL) and Macintosh Common Lisp (MCL). MCLIDE  
follows the swank protocol of emacs SLIME and can potentially be used  
with the same lisp implementations.

Follow the progress of MCLIDE at www.twitter.com/mclide
